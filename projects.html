Community Energy Futures: Using IxD to Study Social Dynamics in Microgrids

UX Research Interaction Design Speculative Design

This project explores how the interaction design of energy sharing apps could influence policy, technical opportunities, and broader social outcomes within microgrids. Proposes multiple prototypes designed as research tools to study resident perceptions of future possibilities. By doing so, they give voice to communities about which energy futures they find preferable. (Research Grant Pending)

(Publication Forthcoming)
