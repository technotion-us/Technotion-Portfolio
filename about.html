About Me

Hi! I'm Jack Bernard, an undergraduate student at the University of Michigan studying Urban Technology. You can learn more about Urban Technology on my blog here.
I'm interested in how technology affects our everyday lives. I have a wide variety of experiences and skills in creating solutions to complex problems with the goal of helping people. At my core I am a curious dot connector, a relentless instigator, an observant firefighter, and an optimistic daydreamer.
